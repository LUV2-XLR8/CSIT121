David Ojabo

Player Profile & Current Contract Details

David Ojabo is a Scottish-Nigerian American football outside linebacker for the Baltimore Ravens of the National Football League. He played college football at Michigan.

David Ojabo signed a 4 year, $7,909,121 contract with the Baltimore Ravens, including a $2,932,088 signing bonus, $4,696,593 guaranteed, and an average annual salary of $1,977,280.
