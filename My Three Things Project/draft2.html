Tyler Linderbaum

Player Profile & Current Contract Details

Tyler Linderbaum is an American football center for the Baltimore Ravens of the National Football League. He played college football at Iowa and was selected in the first round of the 2022 NFL Draft.

Tyler Linderbaum signed a 4 year, $13,238,286 contract with the Baltimore Ravens, including a $6,807,844 signing bonus, $13,238,286 guaranteed, and an average annual salary of $3,309,572.
